Generating those "aha" moments

Most of my developer “aha” moments come away from the computer. Sometimes it happens just minutes after I’ve been banging my head against the wall trying to figure something out. Or, sometimes it happens hours afterwards. At any rate, its made me realize, that if I took more breaks, I would have more “aha” moments, and we all love those. Its those moments that rekindle that spark that can be dampened by deadlines and the daily grind of a developer.

It may seem counter-productive to take more breaks, after all, if you’re not working, you’re not getting anything done, right? I guess this could be true for some, but for me, if I can take a step out of the trenches, the path to solving a problem becomes much more clear.

What do you do when you’re stuck? Take a break? Power through it?
